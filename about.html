About Us

Mission

At Scratchy Nib, our mission is to inspire and empower individuals to explore the world of calligraphy. We strive to provide accessible resources, guidance, and support to beginners and experienced calligraphers alike. Our aim is to nurture a thriving community that celebrates the beauty and intricacies of this timeless art form.

Vision

Our vision is to be the leading online platform for calligraphy enthusiasts worldwide. We envision a space where individuals can connect, learn, and grow together, sharing their knowledge and passion for calligraphy. We aspire to offer a comprehensive range of educational materials, tools, and opportunities for artists to expand their skills and express their creativity.
